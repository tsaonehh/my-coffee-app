Places To Buy Coffee In Gaborone

Featured below are three of my current top coffee spots

1. Mugg 'n Bean

With 3 locations in Gaborone, this is the OG and the blueprint of coffee spots. Find your fix at Sebele Mall, RiverWalk Mall or inside the Airport.

2. Ola Tia Cafe

The new kid on the block! Everyone is buzzing about this new, cute little spot and its aesthetics. Found right inside the new Remmogo Crossing, which is next to Airport Junction Mall.

4. Cappuccinos

Located in the heart of the city right inside Airport Junction Mall, this Italian style restaurant is perfect to grab some cappuccinos, it's in their name really!
